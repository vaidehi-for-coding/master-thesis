You will be interacting with 2 news recommender systems.

  1. You will be presented with a set of news headlines with short descriptions, links to the respective articles (if you're interested in reading further), and a like button.
  2. You will go through the list and choose articles you like by double-clicking on the like button presented beside the article.
  3. You are free to like any number of articles from the list.
  4. Now click ONCE on the "Get Recommendations" button on the bottom right of the page. Give the new page a few seconds to load.
  5. You will be now presented with 10 articles based on your previous choices.
  6. Continue interacting with the system and try to observe what is going on.

At the end of your interaction with both systems, you will have to fill out a small survey and answer questions based on your observations.
