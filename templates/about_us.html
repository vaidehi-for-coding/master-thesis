You will be interacting with 2 news recommender systems - R1 and R2

  1. You will be presented with a set of news headlines with short descriptions, links to the respective articles (if you're interested in reading further), and a like button.
  2. Simply scroll through the list and choose articles you like/catch your eye by DOUBLE-CLICKING on the like button presented beside the article.
  3. LIKE BETWEEN 3-5 ARTICLES FROM THE LIST.
  4. Now click ONCE on the "Get Recommendations" button on the bottom right of the page.
  5. You will now be presented with 10 articles based on your previous choices.
  6. Read the headlines of these articles and again choose between 3-5 articles on your previous choices.
  7. Continue interacting with the system and try to observe what is going on with the recommendations.
  8. You will have to request for recommendations at least 5 times before being allowed to proceed to R2.
  9. Proceed further by clicking on the "Go to R2" button and follow the same process.
  10. At the end of your interaction with both systems, you will have to fill out a small survey and answer questions based on your observations.
  11. Proceed to filling a survey by clicking on the "Go to Survey" button.
  12. A new tab with the survey page will open automatically.

PLEASE DO NOT REFRESH ANY PAGE AS IT WILL START A NEW SESSION.
