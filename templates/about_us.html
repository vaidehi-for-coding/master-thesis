<INSTRUCTIONS>



You will be interacting with 2 news recommendation systems - R1 and R2

  1. You will be presented with a list of 35 news categories. Select at least 7 categories based on your interest.
  2. Based on your chosen categories, you will be presented with a set of news headlines with short descriptions, links to the respective articles (if you're interested in reading further), and a like button.
  3. Simply scroll through the list and choose articles you like/catch your eye by DOUBLE-CLICKING the like button presented beside the article.
  4. Like between 3-5 articles from the list.
  5. Then click on "Get Recommendations" on the bottom right of the page.
  6. You will be presented with 10 articles based on your previously chosen articles.
  7. Read the headlines of these articles and again choose between 3-5 articles that appeal to you.
  8. Pay attention to the articles recommended (headlines/description) everytime.
  9. You will have to request for recommendations at least 5 times before being allowed to proceed to R2.
  10. Proceed further by clicking on the "Go to R2" button.
  11. You will be once again presented with a set of news headlines, same as in step 1.
  12. Follow the same process.
  13. At the end of your interaction with both systems, you will have to fill out a small survey and answer questions based on your observations.
  14. Proceed to filling a survey by clicking on the "Go to Survey" button.
  15. A new tab with the survey page will open automatically.

PLEASE DO NOT REFRESH ANY PAGE AS IT WILL START A NEW SESSION.
